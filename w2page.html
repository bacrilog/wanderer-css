SCARAMOUCHE

Initially conceived by Raiden Ei as a trial for the creation of an impervious puppet
infused with Gnosis, Scaramouche emerged as a successful experiment.
Despite plans to discard him, Ei opted to leave his destiny to fate,
sealing his powers and releasing him from slumber in Shakkei Pavilion.

Reawakened, Scaramouche roamed Inazuma as a regular man until the Fatui took notice.
Modified by Fatui Harbinger Il Dottore, he was enhanced beyond his original design,
earning the moniker "The Balladeer". He emerged as an antagonist in various events,
aiding the Fatui in sowing discord. Following a defeat in Sumeru, he disappeared,
only to resurface erasing his existence to rewrite history.

However, his actions merely led to oblivion rather than altering the past,
leaving him adrift as the Wanderer, yearning for purpose.
